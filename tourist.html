🌍 Tourist Destinations in Cilegon & Beyond

Anyer Beach 🌊

Anyer Beach is one of the most iconic coastal destinations in Banten. With its golden sands, calm waves, and stunning sunsets, it is a favorite for families, couples, and travelers. Local vendors offer fresh seafood dishes, while water sports such as banana boating and jet skiing make it a hub of recreational activities.

Historically, Anyer also holds significance as the starting point of the Great Post Road built by the Dutch during colonial times. Thus, it is not only a leisure destination but also a place of cultural and historical importance.

Kelapa Tujuh Beach 🏝

For those seeking tranquility, Kelapa Tujuh Beach offers a peaceful escape. Unlike the bustling Anyer, this hidden gem is quieter and surrounded by coconut trees, giving it a tropical and secluded atmosphere.

Its serene waves make it ideal for meditation, photography, and relaxation. It is a destination where one can truly disconnect from the urban rush and reconnect with nature.

Sunda Strait Islands 🐠

The Sunda Strait is dotted with small islands, each offering a unique marine experience. Snorkeling and diving around these islands reveal an underwater paradise rich with coral reefs and diverse marine species. The waters are crystal clear, inviting explorers to immerse themselves in nature’s beauty.

These islands are also perfect for camping, fishing, and exploring the coastal lifestyle of local communities. They embody the natural wealth and biodiversity that makes Indonesia extraordinary.

Bukit Teletubbies 🌿

This lush hill area, nicknamed Bukit Teletubbies, is famous for its rolling green landscapes. It offers a scenic backdrop for photography and hiking, providing fresh air and a peaceful escape from the industrial buzz of Cilegon.

Whether for a morning hike or a picnic with loved ones, the gentle slopes and natural beauty make it a favorite among young adventurers and families alike.

Waduk Krakatau Steel 🚤

Originally built as part of industrial infrastructure, Waduk Krakatau Steel has now transformed into a recreational site. Visitors enjoy fishing, boating, or simply strolling around its green surroundings. The artificial lake has become a symbol of how industry and leisure can coexist harmoniously.

For many residents, this reservoir is also a place of daily relaxation, where they can find peace and a slower pace of life away from the factory lines.

Bukit Batu Gambir 🌄

Bukit Batu Gambir offers panoramic views over Cilegon and its surroundings. At sunrise and sunset, the sky paints dramatic colors, making it a haven for photographers and nature lovers. The climb is relatively easy, rewarding visitors with breathtaking sceneries.

It is a place of inspiration, where one can reflect, breathe deeply, and feel the beauty of nature unfolding from the heights.
